Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Description

Breakfast, lunch, dessert, or snack time, any time's the right time for this delicious banana smoothie. This easy smoothie recipe is a 5-star favorite, and it's ready in less than 5 minutes.

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • Â½ cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
  4. Enjoy!
